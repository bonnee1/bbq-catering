Chuckwagon BBQ Catering

Let us cater your next event with our authentic Santa Maria style BBQ. We offer a variety of catering options for groups of 20 and up. We deliver comfort food for your guests with homestyle cooking and on or offsite grilling. We have menu options for all occasions. Our western style bbq wagons set a unique background to food from the the early west. To place an order or schedule a tasting, give us a call, send us an email or fill out the form below.
